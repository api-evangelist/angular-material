This week's community roundup highlights podcast episodes on Angular 20's engineering decisions, Alain Chautard's recap of Angular 21.1 minor features and performance improvements, and Fanis Prodromou's demonstration of combining asynchronous validators with debouncing in Signal Forms to prevent unnecessary API calls. It also features French-language guides on dynamic field lists, an interview with Matthieu Riegler about joining the Angular core team, and Martine Dowden's steps for migrating to modern Control Flow syntax from legacy structural directives.
